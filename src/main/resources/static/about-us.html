About Us

School can be overwhelming and stressful for many students as it tends to feel pressuring with deadlines and assignments that are hard to manage.

Students, like ourselves, stuggle to find resources to help us overcome these stressers in our busy highschool lives. So, we came up with our app, Chatriot.

Our app creats a space where students can intantly connect to their peer through live chat to ask question, find study partners, and share ideas.

We believe learning is better together, and no student should feel like they have to figure out everything on their own.
